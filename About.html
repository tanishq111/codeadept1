With the changes taking place in the campus recruitments, it's high time to get your skills sharpened!


CodeAdept is a core technical event solely conducted by the Department of Information Technology of University Institute of Technology, RGPV which has now become a legacy. The incentives of the competition is to provide a platform for ameriolating your coding skills and to get an idea of the changing recruitment scenario.


CodeAdept and CodeAdept 2.0 was a great success under the guidance of Dr. Ashmita Moghe, Dr. Anjana Pandey, Dr. Roopam Gupta and Dr. Sachin Goyal and under the mentorship of Mr. Vikas Rohit and Mr. Amit Namdev.


The major branches that were involved in the competition were IT, CS, EC and MCA who not only participated with great enthusiasm but also showcased their programming skills.


So here we are back again with CODEADEPT 3.0 to calm your frenzied minds with the same exuberance as last years. Get yourself registered and unleash the geek in you.


Features:

● Get an idea of the changing recruitment scenario.

● Sharpen your coding skills.
